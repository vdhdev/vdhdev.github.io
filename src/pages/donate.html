Thank you for your generous support!

PLEASE HELP VISITING DENTAL HYGIENE, INC CONTINUE TO BE ABLE TO BRING THESE SERVICES TO THOSE WITH LIMITED ACCESS TO DENTAL HYGIENE CARE.

Here’s a current list of needed supplies you may donate to us:

  • gloves
  • masks
  • toothbrushes
  • manual toothbrushes
  • spinbrushes
  • floss
  • floss picks
  • toothpaste
  • dental hygiene instruments
  • prophy paste
  • gallon distilled water
  • paper towel
  • disinfectant wipes

You can help a patient live a longer, healthier life. Since Visiting Dental Hygiene, Inc. launched in 2013, we have seen many uninsured patients, thereby providing free dental care to under-served patients in Southeast, Northeast, Central and parts of Western regions of Massachusetts.

But there are still patients we’re not able to reach…

All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. For more information on donating to Visiting Dental Hygiene, Inc, feel free to email: emmalawson@visitingdentalhygiene.com or call us at 781-267-7627.

We are a federally-recognized 501(c) (3) tax-exempt organization.

If you are unable to pay for dental care, please call us to discuss your situation so we can help you! We care about your health!

Our mobile dental hygiene services are funded in part by a grant from the Blue Cross Blue Shield of Massachusetts Foundation. Created in 2001, the mission of the Blue Cross Blue Shield Massachusetts Foundation is to expand access to health care in Massachusetts through grantmaking and policy initiatives. www.bluecrossmafoundation.org
