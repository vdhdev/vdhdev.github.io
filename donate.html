Programs such as the Visiting Dental Hygiene are often the only resource that many families can rely upon to help cover the cost of dental services so that adults and children alike can have all their treatment needs addressed in a kind and compassionate manner.

“Bringing smiles and dignity to dental care” is not simply a slogan, but a principle we live by. We proudly provide the dental care that underserved communities need – preventive dental care with compassion and comfort for those who truly need it. VDH is here to help.

Your most important asset is your smile.... and despite its impact on the overall health and well-being, many people don’t have access to dental care due to a range of barriers, including mobility, cost, or fear of treatment.

We eliminate these barriers by meeting you where you are, and providing quality, compassionate care where and when you need it regardless of your ability to pay.

Our Core Values

Dental care is overlooked as an important aspect of overall health. VDH believes that everyone deserves the care that will result in beautiful smiles. Getting it changes lives. We recognize that the first step may be the hardest. So, our hygienists provide support and compassion for the whole person.

Please join us in our efforts to bring smiles and dignity to dental care for those who need it.
